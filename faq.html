Welcome


- Do I get Akc Papers If I am getting the Discounted pet price?


We do supply AKC papers for puppies being sold at a pet discount proce. AKC papers will be sent with every puppy that is purchased at Lissasmoyedhomes thanks.


- What do you recommend to help  with potty training ?


We are absolutely firm believers in crate training. Your dog will love you for it! If you aren’t sure what this entails, go to the AKC website and search it. There are several good articles on how to do it properly. We are always here to help our customers. That’s just one of the many reasons you will be glad you bought your puppy from Lissasmoyedhomes.


-How does your waiting list works?


Our waiting list is for clients that for sure want a puppy. You pay the $600 deposit and let us know which sex you are looking for. We’ll notify you when we get puppies that match your request. You may adopt that puppy, or wait for the next litter if you choose. The waiting list orders will be filled on a first come, first served basis. All waiting list customers will have the opportunity to adopt before anyone on the call back list is notified.


-Will someone from Lissasmoyedhomes be available if I have any question?


Absolutely! We are always here to help our customers and friends in any way we can. You will be so glad that you choose to purchase a puppy from us.


-What should I do with my puppy when I get it home?


Do all the normal puppy care but most importantly, bond with your new pup. Yes the puppy has been socialized, but it has also spent the majority of its time with its litter-mates. The puppy will bond with you if you spend time with it the first several days that you receive it. Then it will be your friend forever.


-How do I socialize my puppy?


With 4 children in our family, socialization is not a problem. Not only will your puppy have been around children, but other dogs and cats as well.


-Do you offer shipping?


We can ship your puppy in several different ways. The choice is ultimately up to you. Ground, air cargo, and puppy nanny are all options we are happy to discuss with you. For more on shipping see our “shipping“ page at Lissasmoyedhomes.com


-How can you assure me that I will get a healthy puppy?


We breed only the healthiest breeding of Samoyed puppies which are available and we offer a one year health guarantee on every puppy we sell. See our “Buyers Contract” page. for more.


-How do I pay for my Puppy?


At Lissasmoyedhomes, we do accept payments with Zelle, PayPal,  depending on the available payment method of which the customer has which rhyme with our acceptable forms of payment acceptance.


-Are all of your dogs registered?


Yes all our puppies are. We fell in love with the Samoyed breed years ago and all off our breeding animals are AKC registered. That’s one of the many ways we ensure that you get a healthy puppy to bring you years of love and enjoyment.


-Are these Puppies up to date on thier shots?


Absolutely perfer all puppies are current on their basic puppy vaccines that are appropriate for their age.


-How much is the deposit to reserve a puppy?


The deposit to reserve a puppy is $600. The deposit is non-refundable, unless your puppy would become ill prior to you receiving it.
